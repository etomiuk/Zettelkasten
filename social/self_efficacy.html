Self-Efficacy

Self-efficacy relates to how well you think you can accomplish a task (Lecture 6). Other people can help us increase our self-efficacy by providing mentorship during our learning. One's self-efficacy affects one's behaviour and attitude towards a task (Lecture 6). Those with high self-efficacy will be more likely to accomplisht the task because they are confident in their ability to finish it, and they will work through the challenges that are there. They are also more likely to set harder goals and challenge themselves (Leture 6).
