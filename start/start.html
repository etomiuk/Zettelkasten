Composing your first piece: where to begin?

You are interested and motivated to begin composing your next masterpiece, but you have little knowledge on how to get your creative ideas on paper and how to express yourself musically. Not to worry, this section is here to guide you on different strategies composers might use to generate certain effects, based mostly on cognitive theories of learning to hopefully give some ideas on how you can implement these in your own compositions.
