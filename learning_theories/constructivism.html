Learning Theories

Constructivism

According to constructivists, learning happens by making your own meaning from your experiences (Ertmer & Newby, 2013). Vygostsky's social constructivist theory claims that this knowledge is created as a group - you need the help from others to create knowledge (Lecture 6)
